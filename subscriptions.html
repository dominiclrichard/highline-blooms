Flower Subscriptions: Bring Fresh Blooms to Your Door

A flower subscription, also known as a flower share or flower CSA (Community Supported Agriculture), is a wonderful way to enjoy fresh, locally grown flowers while supporting sustainable farming practices. It’s a win-win for both the consumer and the grower!



How It Works

When you sign up for a flower subscription, you’re committing to receive a weekly or monthly “share” of freshly cut, seasonal flowers. Paid for in advance, your subscription will be delivered to your doorstep or available for pickup on a set schedule. As a subscriber, you’ll receive the freshest, most beautiful blooms each season, all grown right here in Colorado using sustainable practices on our small farm. Unlike most flowers sold in the U.S., which are often imported from countries with questionable labor practices and excessive pesticide use, our flowers are grown locally with transparency and care. By subscribing, you’re connecting directly with our farm and supporting ethical growing practices.


Why Subscriptions Benefit Growers

Flower subscriptions help us plan ahead by securing sales before the season begins. This provides us with the funds needed to purchase seeds, plants, compost, and other supplies to ensure the best start to the growing season. Offering flower shares is an essential part of maintaining a sustainable and thriving farm.
