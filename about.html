Who We Are...

Welcome to Highline Blooms – Where Beauty Grows in Englewood, Colorado

Since 2022, Highline Blooms has been creating vibrant, lovingly cultivated blooms in the heart of Englewood, Colorado. What began as a simple love for flowers has blossomed into a thriving urban flower farm that continues to flourish year after year. With new plots expanding regularly, we're able to experiment with a diverse range of colors, shapes, and techniques to provide our customers with the freshest and most stunning blooms available.

Our Story

When Liz and her husband Bob purchased two acres of land in Englewood, formerly used for boarding horses, Liz saw an opportunity to turn the soil into something special. Although the Colorado clay was challenging to work with, Liz poured her heart into transforming the land, and now it thrives with healthy, vibrant flowers. The farm is home to a variety of blooms, including Yarrow, Sea Holly, Scabiosa, Solidago, Lisianthus, Dahlia, Celosia, Gomphrena, and many more.

For the past 25 years, Liz has worked as a corporate art consultant, helping businesses find distinctive artwork for spaces like conference rooms, offices, receptions, and break areas. While she continues her work as an art consultant, Liz has also developed a passion for growing flowers and creating unique floral arrangements. Much like the artwork she finds for corporate spaces, each floral arrangement is a carefully crafted composition, blending colors, textures, and forms to create something truly one-of-a-kind.

The passion for flowers runs deep for Liz Graham, the heart and soul behind Highline Blooms. Liz grew up surrounded by gardening and the love of nature, as her mother’s green thumb inspired a lifelong connection to the earth. Summers spent in Switzerland with her grandparents—where they cultivated a stunning variety of fruits, vegetables, and flowers—only deepened Liz’s appreciation for all things growing. From picking raspberries and cherries to exploring her grandmother’s beloved rose gardens, Liz’s childhood was a celebration of nature’s wonders.

Our Mission

At Highline Blooms, our mission is simple: to provide a curated collection of high-quality, fresh, and unique flowers grown right here in the Denver area. We are committed to using sustainable practices to create a small, thriving farm that supports both our local community and the environment.
